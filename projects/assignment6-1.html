A.P.J Abdul Kalam

Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist who seerved as the as the 11th president of India from 2002 to 2007. He was born and raised in Rameswarm,TamilNadu and studied physics and aerospace engineering. He is known as the Missile Man of India for his work on the development of ballistic and launch vehicle technology.

Kalam was elected as the 11th president of Inida in 2002 with the support of both the ruling Bharatiya Janata Party and the then-opposition Indian National Congress. widely referred to as the "People's President". He was a recipent of several prestigious awards, including the Bharat Ratna , India's highest civilian honour.

While delevering a lecture at the Indian Institute of Management Shillong , Kalam collapsed and died from an apparent cardiac arrest on 27 July 2015, aged 83.
