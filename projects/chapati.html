recipe for soft chapatis

recipe of chapati


chapati is very famous, well known , common dish in india. It is also known as roti,phulka,safati and many other names.

Ingridients required

ingrident quantity
aata 1 1/2 cup
water as required
saltas required
ghee 1 1/2 table spoon

procedure for soft chapati's

  • take a large sized bowl add 1 1/2 cup of aata into it
  • now add water salt and ghee into it and mix well
  • knead the dough properly
  • make sure the dough is neither thick nor thin by adding water and maintain the correct consistency
  • now make few balls from the prepared dough
  • place the balls on the flat surface and sprinkle some dry flour to it and flatten them using rolling pin
  • add further flour inorder to prevent the rolls from sticking to the surface and make them to a perfect round shape
  • place a pan on the medium flame and put the chapati on the pan
  • cook the chapati on the pan on both sides with the help of a pair of tongs
  • check for the little brown spots, once they appear the chapati will start to puff and gets cooked
  • once the chapati gets cooked completely transfer it to a plate and serve it with any gravy or curry of your choice
